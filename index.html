■ Our Story

MORE THAN A GYM

City De Fitness International was founded in 2018 with one mission: to bring world-class fitness to the heart of Uyo. What started as a small iron paradise has grown into Akwa Ibom's most respected training facility.

We believe fitness is not a luxury — it's a necessity. Whether you're a first-time gym-goer or a seasoned competitor, our doors are open and our coaches are ready to help you become the strongest version of yourself.

Our facility is fully equipped with premium free weights, commercial cardio machines, functional training zones, and dedicated spaces for group classes. Everything you need. Right here in Uyo.

State-of-the-art equipment maintained to international standards

Certified personal trainers with ISSA & NASM credentials

Clean, secure, and air-conditioned training environment

Community of over 500 dedicated members across Uyo and Akwa Ibom
